F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Xpixel unique?

It’s the first large-scale NFT where anyone can own and design a piece of the final artwork, creating a truly collective masterpiece.

How many pixels are there?

Exactly 1,000,000 pixels. Once they’re gone, they’re gone.

Can I choose where my pixels are?

Yes! Our canvas lets you pick an exact spot, and pricing varies based on placement and size.

When does the sale start?

Early access will be given to waitlist members. Join today to secure your place.

Which blockchain will be used?

We’ll announce the chain closer to launch, focusing on low fees, speed, and NFT compatibility.
